Brief Information
About SERTORA 2018

Vision: Nation without natural disaster.

Mission: Provide a disaster management training for Sri Lanka scouts all over the country to identify natural disaster, build awareness among society about natural disaster and used as a resource person in a natural disaster.

Participation: 750 scouts from all over the country.

Date: 21st to 23rd September 2018.

Venue: Lakeview Park International Scout Centre, Dharmaraja College, Kandy Sri Lanka.

More Information Coming Soon!

Event Details:

  • Introduction to disasters.
  • Training session on disaster theory and practical.
  • Special Training on build awareness among people about disasters.
  • Prepare a plan to share the knowledge to other scouts in each scout district with help of Disaster Management Centre.
  • Prepare communication channel with Disaster Management Centre.
  • Special training sessions on disasters.

Resources:

  • Ministry of irrigation and water resource and disaster management.
  • Disaster Management Centre.
  • Ministry of defence.

Main Sessions:

  • Whole Programme Introduction (Organizing Committee)
  • Disasters (DMC)
  • Disaster Management (DMC)
  • As a scout How to Prepare (DMC)
  • Case Study ( Manikfarm or Tsunami or Meeriyabadda or Samasara )(DMC)
  • Awareness and Communication Information Gathering ( DMC)
